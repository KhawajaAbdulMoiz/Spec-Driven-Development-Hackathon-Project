# How to Use This Textbook

This guide will help you get the most out of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook.

## Navigation

- **Modules**: The main navigation shows 5 modules, each focused on a specific area of robotics
- **Chapters**: Each module contains 3-5 chapters that build on each other
- **Sidebar**: Use the left sidebar to navigate between chapters
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific topics

## Chapter Structure

Each chapter follows a consistent structure:

### 1. **Learning Objectives**
The chapter starts with clear learning goals. Review these to understand what you'll learn.

### 2. **Main Content**
The core material is presented in digestible sections with:
- Clear explanations (written for beginners)
- Key concepts highlighted
- Real-world examples
- Diagrams and visualizations

### 3. **Did You Know?**
Interesting facts and trivia related to the chapter topic.

### 4. **Real-World Examples**
Practical applications featuring actual robots:
- Boston Dynamics Spot
- Tesla Optimus
- ABB collaborative robots
- And many more!

### 5. **Key Takeaways**
A summary of the most important concepts in the chapter.

### 6. **Try It!**
Hands-on activities you can try:
- Simulation exercises
- Thought experiments
- Browser-based activities
- No special hardware needed

### 7. **Exercises**
Questions to test your understanding:
- Multiple choice
- Short answer
- Problem-solving challenges

### 8. **Further Reading**
Links to related chapters and external resources for deeper learning.
