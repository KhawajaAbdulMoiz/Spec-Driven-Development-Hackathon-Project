# About This Textbook

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook** ‚Äî an open, free, beginner-friendly learning resource for anyone interested in understanding robotics from first principles.

## Our Mission

We believe robotics and physical AI should be accessible to everyone. This textbook breaks down complex robotics concepts into clear, structured lessons with real-world examples and hands-on activities.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook covers five core modules:

1. **Module 1: Foundations of Robotics** - Core concepts, terminology, and the history of robots
2. **Module 2: Perception and Sensing** - How robots see, hear, and sense their environment
3. **Module 3: Control and Motion Planning** - How robots move and make decisions
4. **Module 4: AI Agents in Robotics** - Machine learning and autonomous behavior
5. **Module 5: Real-World Applications & Ethics** - Practical applications and responsible AI

## How to Use This Textbook

- **For Learners**: Start with Module 1 and progress sequentially. Each chapter builds on the previous one.
- **For Educators**: Use the chapter structure, exercises, and "Try It" activities for classroom instruction.
- **For Practitioners**: Jump to relevant modules based on your needs.
