# Chapter 1: What is Physical AI?

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will:
- Understand what physical AI means
- Learn the difference between software AI and physical AI
- Recognize real examples of physical AI systems
- Understand why physical AI is important

## Introduction

Physical AI is artificial intelligence that interacts with the physical world. Unlike traditional software that operates on computers, physical AI systems use sensors, actuators, and control systems to perceive and act in the real world.
